CHICKEN KARAHI


Karahi Gosht, either lamb or mutton or chicken is Pakistani traditional curry with tomato-based gravy and is made in a narrow-based wok. Indian spell it as Kadhai where letter 'dh' gives the sound of the letter 'R' with the round mouth.


INGREDIANTS

  • 1/2 kilograms mutton
  • 2 tomato
  • 14 large green chilli
  • 3 medium onion
  • 4 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 bunch coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste

STEPS

  1. PBoil meat with 2 cups of water and ginger and garlic paste on a medium flame.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and put in onions. Fry till golden and then add tomatoes.
  3. Now add 1 tsp salt, 1 and 1/2 tsp red chili powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp coriander powder.
  4. Add the meat, fry for 20 minutes on high flame till water from tomatoes dries and a little gravy is left.
